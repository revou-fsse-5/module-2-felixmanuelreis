About Us

Grisenfor plastic waste management center is dedicated to empowering coastal and riverbank communities through sustainable waste management solutions.

Based on the innovative waste bank concept, we focus on collecting and recycling plastic trash, fostering environmental stewardship and economic opportunities.

By targeting women and young people, we aim to create a cleaner, healthier environment while providing valuable skills and income-generating activities, transforming waste into a resource that benefits both the community and the planet.

Our Services

Plastic Collection and Recycling: We offer efficient plastic waste collection services from households and businesses along coastal and riverbank communities. Our recycling process ensures that plastic waste is transformed into reusable materials, reducing environmental impact.

Community Education and Awareness: We conduct workshops and educational programs to raise awareness about the importance of plastic waste management. Our initiatives aim to educate community members on how to reduce, reuse, and recycle plastic waste effectively.

Women and Youth Empowerment: We provide training and support to women and young people, equipping them with the skills needed to participate in and benefit from our waste management programs. This includes income-generating opportunities through plastic collection and recycling activities.
